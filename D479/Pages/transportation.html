Air Travel

Most travelers arrive by air at our island's cozy airport, designed for small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is currently working on expanding it to welcome larger jets, with the project set to finish within the next few years.

Ground Travel

In Taniti City, public buses operate daily from early morning until late evening, offering convenient travel options. Private buses serve areas outside the city, and taxis are also available. For those who prefer driving, rental cars can be found from several vendors, with helmets required by law. Thanks to its flat landscape, Taniti City is very walkable, and many visitors near Merriton Landing enjoy discovering the area on foot.
